# WorldFlows - Your Smarter Way to Travel and Connect

## Imagine this:
You decide to travel.  
Instead of opening 10 tabs, comparing random blog lists, and juggling map pins, you open **WorldFlows**.

In just a few steps, the app turns your destination, budget, travel dates, and interests into a **personalized day-wise plan** you can actually use.

And it does not stop there.

WorldFlows also helps you meet people with the same vibe through **Zelo** - our social discovery layer for real-world, intent-based meetups.

## Why users love WorldFlows

- **Plan faster**: AI-assisted itinerary generation removes decision fatigue.
- **Travel smarter**: Suggestions are shaped by your preferences, not generic lists.
- **Discover more**: Hidden gems and local-style experiences go beyond obvious spots.
- **Connect in real life**: Use Zelo to create or join meetups around shared travel intent.
- **Stay organized**: Manage saved trips, trip details, preferences, and profile in one place.

## What makes WorldFlows different

### 1) AI Planning That Feels Personal
WorldFlows uses a structured trip questionnaire (destination, timing, budget, interests, and more) to build a plan that feels handcrafted for you.

No copy-paste itinerary.  
No one-size-fits-all travel guide.  
Just a flow that adapts to *your* style.

### 2) Zelo: Travel Meets Social
With Zelo, travel is no longer solo unless you want it to be.

You can:
- create intent-based places (like "sunset walk" or "grab coffee"),
- discover nearby places posted by others,
- join with tokens,
- chat with participants, and coordinate with confidence.

### 3) Built for Real Travelers
From first login to post-trip planning, WorldFlows covers:
- auth and profile onboarding,
- planning and generation screens,
- itinerary and saved trips,
- notifications and privacy/security controls,
- premium/token pathways for deeper usage.
