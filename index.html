What WorldFlows Includes

The app is built as a full travel lifecycle platform: pre-trip planning, itinerary execution, social participation, and post-trip management.

Smart Trip Planner

Destination search, date selection, budget preferences, and interests drive a personalized itinerary.

Zelo Social Layer

Create or join intent-based meetups on a map, then coordinate with participants through chat.

Hidden Gems

Additional curated exploration areas and destination content beyond the core itinerary.

Saved & Account Tools

Saved trips, profile controls, privacy/security settings, notification controls, and support screens.

How The App Works

1

Sign in, register, or continue as guest, then complete your profile.

2

Use the questionnaire to enter destination, dates, budget, and interests.

3

Generate your day-wise travel plan with AI-assisted recommendations.

4

Review itinerary output, revisit saved trips, and open related trip screens.

5

Use Zelo to host or join local meetups and chat with participants.
