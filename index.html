What is WorldFlows?

WorldFlows is a platform for planning trips with clarity. You share destination, style, and preferences, and the app helps build an itinerary with practical suggestions you can save, share, and revisit.

What is Zelo?

Zelo is the social layer of WorldFlows. Create an intent-based meetup at a location, or join one that matches your vibe. You can chat with participants and coordinate quickly.

Zelo tokens

Tokens keep interactions meaningful and reduce spam in social flows.

Listing Token

Use 1 listing token to create a new Zelo place.

Joinable Token

Use 1 joinable token to join someone else's place.

How to Use WorldFlows

  1. Set your destination and travel preferences.
  2. Generate and save your itinerary.
  3. Open Zelo to create or join nearby meetups.
  4. Chat with participants and coordinate safely.

Privacy Policy (Quick View)

We collect only the data needed to provide planning, social, and location-based features. We use secure infrastructure, do not sell your personal data, and let you control key privacy settings in-app.
